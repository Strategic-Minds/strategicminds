# Strategic Minds Advisory Brand Pack

## Brand Core

- Name: Strategic Minds Advisory
- Short name: Strategic Minds
- Tagline: Intelligence In Motion
- Category: AI-powered advisory and automation studio
- Audience: founders, service business owners, operators, and creators turning expertise into systems
- Promise: turn ideas into automated, AI-powered business systems with governance, approval gates, and preview-first execution

## Positioning

Strategic Minds Advisory is a premium operating partner for businesses that need practical AI systems, not gimmicks. The brand should feel strategic, precise, controlled, and commercially useful.

The website should communicate:

- AI-powered business system design
- Automated workflows and client portals
- Approval-gated operations
- Safe test-mode payments
- Preview-first builds and rollback discipline
- Human advisory judgment behind automation

## Voice

Use a tone that is:

- strategic
- clear
- premium
- calm
- operator-grade
- direct

Avoid:

- hype
- guaranteed income claims
- generic AI buzzword stacking
- cartoon robots
- fake customer logos
- fake analytics wins

## Visual Identity

Core palette:

- Ink: `#0B1220`
- Charcoal: `#151A23`
- White: `#FFFFFF`
- Mist: `#F5F7FA`
- Steel: `#64748B`
- Electric blue: `#2563EB`
- Cyan: `#06B6D4`
- Success: `#16A34A`
- Warning: `#F59E0B`

Palette rules:

- Use ink, white, and mist as the main system palette.
- Use electric blue and cyan as precision accents.
- Avoid purple-heavy, beige, tan, brown, orange, orb, blob, or one-hue themes.

Typography:

- Primary: Geist Sans or system sans-serif
- Numeric/code/status: Geist Mono or system monospace
- Letter spacing: 0
- Hero type only on real hero sections
- Dashboard/admin type should be compact and scannable

## Layout Rules

- Radius: 8px or less
- Avoid nested cards
- Use cards only for repeated objects, modals, or framed tools
- Public website sections should be full-width bands or constrained unframed layouts
- Client/admin surfaces should be dense, restrained, and operational
- First viewport must clearly signal Strategic Minds Advisory and business-system automation

## Primary CTAs

- Schedule a Call
- View Packages
- Start Client Intake

## Route Family Treatment

Public routes:

- premium advisory website
- clear offers, process, trust, and intake
- real/generated imagery showing business systems and operators

Client routes:

- secure client operating dashboard
- journey, projects, documents, payments, invoices, meetings, messages, AI assistant, settings
- placeholder data only in generated images

Admin routes:

- restrained control plane
- approvals, clients, projects, payments, invoices, workflows, agents, reports, receipts, releases, settings
- governance, rollback, and receipt signals visible

## Safety And Governance Signals

Always preserve these ideas in copy and UI:

- approval gates
- preview-first builds
- test-mode payments
- rollback receipts
- AI refusal rules
- no production mutation without approval
- no secrets exposure
